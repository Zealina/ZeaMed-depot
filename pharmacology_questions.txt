Q: What is the mechanism of action of aspirin?
Answer: Inhibits cyclooxygenase
Options: [Inhibits cyclooxygenase, Blocks acetylcholine, Activates dopamine, Increases serotonin]
Explanation: Aspirin irreversibly inhibits COX-1 and COX-2 enzymes, leading to decreased formation of prostaglandins.

Q: Which drug is used to treat hypertension?
Answer: Lisinopril
Options: [Lisinopril, Metformin, Atorvastatin, Furosemide]
Explanation: Lisinopril is an ACE inhibitor that helps relax blood vessels.

Q: What is the antidote for heparin overdose?
Answer: Protamine sulfate
Options: [Protamine sulfate, Vitamin K, FFP, Desmopressin]
Explanation: Protamine sulfate binds to heparin to form a stable complex, neutralizing its anticoagulant effects.
